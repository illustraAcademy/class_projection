Ice breaker question

What three things would you do if you were invisible?

Don't forget the metrics! 45 sec, 8-12 sentences, Give reasons why

  • Listen to the question carefully.
  • Think of a short and easy-to-understand answer.
  • Share your answer with the group.
  • Be friendly and open to hearing others' answers.
  • Enjoy the conversation and have fun getting to know the others!
